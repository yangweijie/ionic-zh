Brighton Resort

Planning your day on the hill shouldn’t require 6 different apps that take up a gig of your phone’s space. Thanks to the new Brighton Resort app, it no longer has to. With all the info you’ll ever need to go big, wrapped up in one tight package of interactive eye candy, we’ll make sure that your trusty pocket genius has plenty of room to process a #selfie or two when you’re hitching a lift to the top.

The app features up-to-date snow totals, a 5-day forecast, lift and run info, road conditions, and a photo gallery.

Cruise Monkey

CruiseMonkey is an app for JoCo Cruise Crazy, a cruise organized by Jonathan Coulton which is a bit like PAX on a ship, with nerdy musicians, gaming, and lots of community events.

The app has maps of the ship, a list of amenities, and then lists the events scheduled for JoCo Cruise Crazy, but since a large part of the cruise is people self-organizing to do cool things, it also lets you publish your own events to your shipmates, and they can favorite them along with the official events.

We also have our own onboard twitter clone called "Twit-Arr" which is used for communication between cruisers. CruiseMonkey has the ability to view images posted to Twit-Arr in a handy browser, as well as notify you when you have new "Seamail".
